How to Send a Message to Your Monitor on your iPhone
-----------------------------------

While you’re out and about during your shift you might need to send some information to your monitor.
 - The route plan for your journey –
 - Your mileage –
 - Information about your specific location if you are going out of network coverage –
 - A change to your planned schedule –
 - Or many, many other reasons

How to send a Message:
1. At the top of the screen are 3 lines. Tap them to open the side menu. Choose ‘Send a Message’.
2. Your screen now says ‘Add a message’ above a large white box. Tap anywhere in the box. Type your message using the keyboard on your screen.
3. Once you have finished writing, tap the orange ‘Send Message’ button.
